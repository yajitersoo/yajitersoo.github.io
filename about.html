Professional Summary

A technical profile grounded in information management, analytics, and operational use of data.

My work focuses on translating fragmented operational data into structured systems, dashboards, maps, analytical products, and reporting outputs that strengthen visibility, accountability, planning, and decision support.

Across humanitarian and technical contexts, I have worked with reporting systems, partner data flows, geospatial products, dashboard development, automation workflows, and analytical tools that improve the consistency and usability of data for teams and decision-makers.

I am particularly interested in analytical environments where data quality, structured workflows, and visual outputs come together to support more coherent operational understanding.
