About

I work at the intersection of information management, analytics, reporting systems, and geospatial decision support.

My work is focused on turning fragmented operational data into structured information products that support coordination, planning, monitoring, and evidence-based action. Across humanitarian and development contexts, I design systems that improve how data is collected, validated, analyzed, visualized, and used.

I am particularly interested in building analytical environments that are practical, scalable, and decision-oriented. This includes reporting workflows, dashboards, GIS outputs, automation pipelines, and technical structures that strengthen both data quality and operational visibility.

Core Focus Areas

  • Information management systems
  • Dashboards and reporting workflows
  • GIS and spatial analysis
  • Workflow automation and data quality
